Hey there! Thanks for checking out Audspark. If you've used Spark you know how easy it makes staying in sync with your team and customers. If you're new to Spark, go create an account and come back when your finished and get a whole lot of new experiences. AudSpark is a product which provides the functionality of voice commands over the Cisco Spark, such as creating rooms, adding members to the rooms etc, all just with the help of the voice.
